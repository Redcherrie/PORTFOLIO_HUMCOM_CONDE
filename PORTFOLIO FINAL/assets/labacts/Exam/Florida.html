    Keylime Pie
  • This beloved dessert, made from Key lime juice, sweetened condensed milk, and egg yolks, encapsulates the essence of the Keys. The pie's origins are tied to local legend, with one story crediting its creation to Aunt Sally, a cook for William Curry, one of Florida's first millionaires.
